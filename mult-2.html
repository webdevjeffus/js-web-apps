Instructions

Mathemagic Multiplication is a multiplication-facts practice game. The computer will offer you a fact set to start with, but you can choose another set by clicking a blue Fact Set button. Type your answer in the white box, and click Submit to enter it into the computer.

You must solve all ten facts in a set before moving on to another set. The facts you've gotten right will be marked with green in the Factors box. When you make a mistake, the Factor will be marked red, and you will receive one Strike. If you receive three Strikes, you're out, and you'll have to start a new fact set.
